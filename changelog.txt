Version: 0.2.0
Date: 2026-01-11
  Major Features:
    - the alternate selection option (shift + left click by default) now clears only corpses.

    - Added instructions to lawnmower shortcut.
  Bugfixes:
    - fixed issue with minable corpses not dropping any items on the ground when cleared, even when
      the setting for it is enabled.
